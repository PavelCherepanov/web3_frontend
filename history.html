History


Birth of Bitcoin

The Bitcoin blockchain was officially launched when the first Bitcoin block, the genesis block, was created on Jan. 3, 2009. is a decentralized digital currency that can be transferred on the peer-to-peer bitcoin network.

GPU Mining

Ordinary CPUs were no longer able to run at the speed needed to meet the increased difficulty of the mining algorithm. So in 2010, there was a release of the first software design specifically for mining with a computer graphics card.

Silk Road

Silk Road was a dark web market where people could buy various illegal and questionable goods. Bitcoin was their preferred payment method because it was decentralized and anonymous.

Mt. Gox

Mt. Gox was a bitcoin exchange based in Shibuya, Tokyo, Japan. Launched in 2010, it was handling over 70% of all bitcoin (BTC) transactions worldwide by early 2014

Bitcoin Cash

As bitcoin grew, its community began to split. There were quite a few users and developers who wanted to increase the block size from 1MB to 8MB, while others wanted to apply a scaling solution called SegWit to Bitcoin.
